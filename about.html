about me

Hello, my name is Elizabeth Reznik, but I go by Liz! I've been in the Web Design Program for the past three years, and while I’ve enjoyed exploring the creative side of technology, I’m excited to take a new path this fall as I head to Rutgers to study Criminal Justice. My goal is to go to law school and eventually become a paralegal. I dream of living in Manhattan—I love the energy of the city. Outside of school, I enjoy spending time at the gym, going to the beach, hanging out with my friends, boyfriend, and family (including my twin brother!), and relaxing with a good romance novel or a creepy horror movie. I also have a five-year-old Scottish Terrier named Conan who definitely keeps life interesting!
